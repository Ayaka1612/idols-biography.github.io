Early Life

Lana Del Rey was born on June 21, 1985, in New York City. Her real name is Elizabeth Woolridge Grant. She grew up in a family that loved music and arts. As a child, Lana was interested in singing and listening to different kinds of music. She started singing and writing songs when she was young.


Music Career
