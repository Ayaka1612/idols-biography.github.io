Early Life & Education

Born in Guiyang, Guizhou Province, Young Captain dropped out in junior high and later studied fashion design at a vocational school. During this time, he taught himself music production using online tutorials at internet cafes.


Start in Music Production

He began by mixing songs for online singers, handling hundreds of orders monthly. With few local opportunities, he moved to Guangzhou at 19 to explore pop music, later returning to Guiyang to build his own recording studio.


Debut & Breakthrough

In 2017, he released his debut single “You Are Everywhere”, which gained wide attention. His 2021 album “Captain’s Music World” and the 2022 viral hit “NUNA” helped him rise to national fame.


Musical Growth

He followed up with notable singles like “For You” and “11”, and released a second album “Walking Here” in late 2022, showcasing his evolving sound in R&B and pop.


Live Performances & Festivals

From late 2022 through 2023, he performed at major music festivals across China, including the National Music Carnival, Wheatfield Music Weekend, and LMF Music Festival, solidifying his place in the live music scene.
